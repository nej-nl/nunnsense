About the Artist
Sue Nunn

I love taking found objects and turning them into something creative that will make people smile or maybe even creep them out a little.

Thrift stores, flea markets and estate sales are some of my favorite places to find material to craft with, because of this obsession I have drawers, boxes and shelves full of smalls and just fun stuff.

I love dabbling in all aspects of art, assemblage is my favorite at the moment.
